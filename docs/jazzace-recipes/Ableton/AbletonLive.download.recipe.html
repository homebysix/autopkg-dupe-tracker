Desciption Downloads the most recent version of Ableton Live based on the version and edition you specify. LIVE_VERS is the major version of Live (9 or later). Possible values for EDITION are: trial, lite, intro, standard, and suite. This recipe now supports Ableton's various ARCHITECTURE options. Valid options for Live 11 and later are "universal" and "intel"; earlier versions require "64".
