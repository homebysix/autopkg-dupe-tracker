Desciption Downloads the latest version of a particular MAJOR_VERSION of Cocktail. MAJOR_VERSION should correspond to a macOS release version. Supported values include: - 26 for Tahoe (default) - 15 for Sequoia - 14 for Sonoma - 13 for Ventura - 12 for Monterey - 11 for Big Sur - 10.15 for Catalina - 10.14 for Mojave - 10.13 for High Sierra - 10.12 for Sierra - 10.11 for El Capitan - 10.10 for Yosemite This recipe does not support older major versions than Yosemite.
