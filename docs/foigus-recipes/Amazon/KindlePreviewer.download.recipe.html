Desciption Downloads latest Kindle Previewer disk image. NOTE: The Kindle Previewer postinstall script (at least as of 2.941) is the following: #### #!/bin/sh sudo chmod -R 755 /Applications/Kindle\ Previewer.app sudo chown -R $USER /Applications/Kindle\ Previewer.app #### And Kindle Previewer, when running, writes files and directories under: /Applications/Kindle Previewer.app/Contents/MacOS Depending on your management system, you may need to address this issue.
