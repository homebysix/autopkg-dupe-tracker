Desciption Downloads the major version of Parallels Desktop specified by MAJOR_VERSION, creates a package, and imports it into your JSS. This recipe has been tested with major versions 9 through 16. For major versions 9 through 15, leave the PLATFORM_ARCH blank in your override. For major version 16, set the PLATFORM_ARCH in your override to either "intel" or "m1" depending on your desired architecture.
