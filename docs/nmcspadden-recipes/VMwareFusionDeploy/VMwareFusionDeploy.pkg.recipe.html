Desciption Creates a VMwareFusion mass deployment package DMG. Requires justinrummel-recipes to download. You can substitute your own deploy.ini file in an override by placing the content in the DEPLOY_INI_FILE input variable. At a minimum, you'll need to put your license key in, or the postflight will fail.
