AdobeReader.pkg.recipe

Last refresh: 2021-12-27 00:09:11

Desciption: Downloads the current Adobe Reader pkg and repackages, replacing a problematic preinstall script, and enabling installation on non-boot volumes. Setting MAJOR_VERSION to "10" will cause a download of the latest/last version of Adobe Reader 10, but the repackager is unlikely to operate properly with that version. This recipe is a retooling of the AdobeReader recipe provided by autopkg. All of the base work goes to them, I just wanted to tweak it a little and get the version number on the installer.
