Desciption Downloads the latest Adobe Reader and imports into Munki. For OS_VERSION, 'Mac OS 10.15.0' or 'Mac OS 12.0' are valid, not 10.15 or 12.0 Additional notes: - Reader ships as an installer pkg that deploys a .app - Due to name changes, we add multiple variations on .app names to blocking_applications. - An installs array is also generated for the .app. - The preinstall_script is there to get around an oversight in the package's preflight script, looking to find a user's trash folder to copy the .app into.
