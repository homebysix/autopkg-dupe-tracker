Desciption Downloads the current Adobe Reader pkg and repackages, replacing a problematic preinstall script, and enabling installation on non-boot volumes. Finally, it is installed. Setting MAJOR_VERSION to "10" will cause a download of the latest/last version of Adobe Reader 10, but the repackager is unlikely to operate properly with that version.
