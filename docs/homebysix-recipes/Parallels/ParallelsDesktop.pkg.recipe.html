Desciption Downloads the major version of Parallels Desktop specified by MAJOR_VERSION and creates a package. This recipe has been tested with major versions 10 through 18. This recipe differs from the one in keeleysam-recipes because it offers code signature verification, does not require a ParallelsURLProvider processor, and also includes pkg and install recipes. Input variable tips: - For major versions 10 through 15 or 17 through 18, leave the PLATFORM_ARCH blank in your override. - For major version 16, set the PLATFORM_ARCH in your override to either "intel" or "m1" depending on your desired architecture.
